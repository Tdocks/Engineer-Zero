# Role Concepts Library

The Role Concepts Library is deliberately different from a lab. Its purpose is to help a learner sound informed, ask sharper questions, recognize technical and organizational boundaries, and know when to partner with a specialist.

Every card is **Know** evidence only. It can improve conceptual readiness, but it never counts as independent building, troubleshooting, or certification evidence.

## Six concept clusters

1. Computing and systems
2. Application engineering
3. Applied AI
4. Safe enterprise AI
5. Delivery and operations
6. Architecture vocabulary

Each card contains a plain-language explanation, role relevance, a fictional example, a red flag, an accountable owner, and a scenario-based knowledge check. The source record attached to the card identifies which factual claim the source supports; it does not pretend that an official source endorses the course’s fictional scenario or teaching format.
